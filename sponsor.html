    VIVAI Integrations:

    Our AI chatbot integrates with platforms like Telegram, Zendesk, live chat, and more. We are eager to explore new integrations and adapt the solution to your preferred communication channels with clients. Let us know your requirements and demands, and we can further examine the ideas and keep you informed of the possibilities!

    Training Data & Transfer to a Live Agent:

    The bot becomes highly proficient in addressing various inquiries by being trained with the right data, which we make hassle-free for you. If the questions are unique or if the business prefers to transfer the chat in special cases, we train our bot to do so. Our back office enables a live agent to easily continue the conversation with the client. We have multiple features that make the life of the agent easier, such as Auto-Translation, AI helper integration, and Generating Chat Summaries.
    Our solution is a great tool that combines the efforts of agents with AI

    Our AI chatbot has improved with natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning, understanding complex queries and providing personalized responses. Emotion recognition is now better than ever.     As chatbot AI evolves, it's inevitable that customer support will be enhanced by AI tools. That's why our solution can easily adapt to business needs and the best current LLM on the market. We have the capability to switch to any suitable AI model (Grok, Llama, Gemini, Copilot, etc).
